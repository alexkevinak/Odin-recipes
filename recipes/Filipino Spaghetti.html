Filipino Spaghetti

Ingredient

  • 2 pounds spaghetti
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 head garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • pound ground beef
  • 1 pound ground pork
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 (26.5 ounce) can spaghetti sauce
  • 1 (14 ounce) jar banana ketchup
  • ¼ cup white sugar
  • ½ cup water
  • 1 pound hot dogs, sliced diagonally
  • ½ cup shredded Cheddar cheese
